Our Services

We offer end-to-end data solutions from developing datawarehouse solutions to coaching data storytelling

Discovery

Through interviews and observations, we learn first hand our client's pain points. We run faciliated interactive sessions with stakeholders to identify and prioritize problems we want to solve.

Platform

We develop the end-to-end infrastructure required for data collection, processing, storage, and analytics. Keeping Master Data Management close to our design process, we build solutions that teams can easily find out what data is available and how to use them.

Storytelling

Each dashboard is a product in its own. Hence we take a product development approach and pair with client's analysts and engineers to develop visually appealling reports and dashboards that provide actionable insight.

Governance

Armed with learnings from Discovery and feedback for the dashboards, we work closely with client's management teams to refactor business practices and processes to ensure we obtain high data quality sustainably.
